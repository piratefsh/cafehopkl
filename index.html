Mapping Unique Cafes around KL

Love coffee? You've come to the right place.

CafeHop KL is a mapping project mapping independent cafes around Kuala Lumpur. We have compiled a map of the best cafes in the Klang Valley, Malaysia for your cafe hopping adventures.

So where is this magical map? It's on this site, and starting 21st Sept 2013, we will have printed copies distributed among the 28 cafes that have supported this project. Check them out!
