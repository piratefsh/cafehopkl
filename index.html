Welcome to Café Hop KL

We're a group of café lovers who want to map out all the unique cafés in and around Klang Valley to share out. We already have a Google Map of these cafés and will be producing a physical map and a full fledged website with the map, blog and more soon. We're working with one of Malaysia's largest trainers of baristas, Barista Guild Asia to make this project happen. For more details, contact us at cafehop@gmail.com
