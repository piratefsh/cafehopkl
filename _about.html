About CafeHop KL

CafeHop KL is a mapping project mapping independent cafes around Kuala Lumpur. We have made this site and also printed copies to distribute among 28 cafes that have supported this project.
Cafes are chosen based on a combination of good coffee, good food, good service and/or good vibes in general. So, what are you waiting for? Start cafe hopping, pop by these novel cafes and ﬁnd one that suits you. We hope you ﬁnd a nice place and have a great coffee.
